SAFETY GUIDELINES

For Delhi Monsoon & Water-Logging

Stay informed. Stay alert. Stay safe.
Avoid flooded streets & underpasses
Beware of open drains & uncovered manholes
Do not touch electric poles or loose wires
Avoid walking in flowing water
Avoid water-logged underpasses (marked on map)
Do not drive if water crosses wheel level
Follow traffic advisories & diversions
Switch off engine if car stalls in water
Keep nearby drains free of waste
Secure electrical appliances
Store emergency numbers
Report persistent water-logging via MCD/DJB
Report New Issue
Mandatory PPE (gumboots, gloves, reflective jackets)
Avoid drain work during peak rainfall
Switch off power before operations
Follow departmental SOPs
